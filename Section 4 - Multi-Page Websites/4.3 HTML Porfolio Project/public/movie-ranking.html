The Best Movies According to Quentin

My top 3 sci-fi movies of all-time.

Star Wars

Tell's a story about the philosophy of the what it takes to become a Jedi Knight and consequences of controlling one's anger to prevent crossing over to the Dark Side.

Back to the Future

Interesting story about a boy's desire to go back in past in order to to change the future of his family.

The Matrix

Deals with concept of a simulated life, i.e. is the world we are living in a simulation or the true reality.
